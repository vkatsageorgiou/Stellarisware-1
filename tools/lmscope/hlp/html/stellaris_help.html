Help Control Options

The following options are available in the "Help" group of controls accessed via the qs-scope menu:

Help "Show..." will replace the waveform display with information about the oscilloscope connector pins found immediately above the CSTN display. "Hide..." will remove this information and revert to the waveform display.
Channel 1 "Find..." will analyze the last data set captured for channel 1 and automatically adjust the scaling and vertical position to move the waveform to the center of the display.
Channel 2 "Find..." will analyze the last data set captured for channel 1 and automatically adjust the scaling and vertical position to move the waveform to the center of the display.
